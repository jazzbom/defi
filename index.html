Hello There

We Are

We're building the next generation of real estate platform for homebuyers who fall short of qualifying for traditional mortgages. We do so by purchasing homes outright and allowing the buyer to build up ownership equity over a 3-4-year lease.

Own it, not rent it.

How it works

1

Apply online

Register your profile and get pre-qualified for a budget.

2

Find your dream home

Work with us to find your desired property or connect us with a seller of your choice.

3

SuchHomes buys the home

We purchase the home outright with cash and you just need to put in a 2-3% downpayment.

4

Move-In, Sublet and start saving

Start living in your dream home or sublet it to your acquaintances. Make timely rental payments whilst gradually building ownership equity and credit score.

5

Buy or sell your equity savings

Qualify for a mortgage and buy back the property from us using the cashback from your built-up equity savings or simply walk away with cash equal to your equity %.
