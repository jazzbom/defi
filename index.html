Hello There

We Are

We're building the next generation of decentralised lending protocol connecting home loans directly to investors.
As a decentralised platform, BlockSourced's mission is to empower its protocol development through a democratic process bestowed by the voting power of BSGov token holders.

How it works

1

Apply online

Register your profile with the desired mortgage request details.

2

KYC/AML

Get a unique NFT token representing your pseudo-identity and credit score upon successful verification at our trusted blockchain-enabled partner service built by our team.

3

Find your dream home

Work with us to find your desired property or connect us with a seller of your choice.

4

Listing & crowdfunding

Pseudonymously list your mortgage crowdfunding request on our decentralised platform and ensure you make a suitable downpayment to attract investors.

5

Auction

The interested investors either agree to fund $X amount at your terms, or submits different terms & over 24 hours the marketplace comes up with an agreed-upon rate and payment terms for this particular loan, and it is presented to you.

6

Acceptance and offer

Once funding is secured, we'll go through the home buying process together i.e. offer acceptance, legal documents and transfer the funds to the seller.

7

Post-trade

Upon a successful purchase, the investors can claim a freely tradable/transferrable NFT attached to this particular mortgage. The interoperable beauty of blockchain is such that the NFT's can be used to unlock secondary liquidity within 3rd party DeFi lending protocols.

8

Repayments & investor

The new homeowner makes regular repayments directly to the decentralised smart contract in the supported native currency of the blockchain in use. The investor who holds the mortgage NFT can claim the available repayments.

Technical stuff

Each new mortgage runs independently on our smart contract protocol deployed on an EVM compatible chain. Our first supported chain will be the stable coin Ethereum side chain xDai, followed closely by Binance Smart Chain.
The treasury within the smart contract is non-custodial which means no one apart from you have access to your funds i.e. not even BlockSourced has access. The investor/buyer can stop the mortgage process by withdrawing their deposits anytime before the contract is set to Sold and funds transferred to the seller by us.
