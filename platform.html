BlockSourced Digital Eco-system

An ecosystem that reduces costs, automates manual interventions, increases operational efficiencies & enables secondary market liquidity through innovative blockchain technologies in the capital formation process.

The Problem

The capital formation process is a time-consuming, tedious & expensive undertaking for any startup or private company wishing to raise money through traditional financing channels. Underwriters, law firms, transfer agents, custodians & other capital advisors all have their own processes & internal systems for structuring deal terms, distribution & cap table management. Meanwhile, the issuer has the onerous task of playing “middleman” to all of these service providers. This unwieldy process ultimately still leaves founders & early-stage investors left with no real sense of ownership, is mired in its own opacity & lacks any sense of liquidity.

The Solution

The BlockSourced platform eliminates the most glaring pain points of the capital formation process - time, opacity, compliance, paperwork, illiquidity & trust - bringing together issuers, investors, capital service providers & investor relations into a “single source of truth” distributed ledger ecosystem on both the public & permissioned privacy-focused blockchain. Armed with our secure & scalable system, all stakeholders within the capital formation lifecycle are able to work collaboratively & efficiently, putting their time & efforts back into their business, rather than stuck in process & bottlenecks.
